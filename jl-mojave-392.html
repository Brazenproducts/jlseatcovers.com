Mojave & 392 Edition — Unique Front Seats

The Jeep Wrangler Mojave and 392 editions come with unique front seats that differ from standard JL/JLU seats. The seat bolsters, cushion shape, and mounting geometry are different enough that covers designed for standard JL seats will not fit correctly.

What Makes Mojave/392 Front Seats Different?

  • Mojave: Desert-rated suspension and unique interior touches, including differently bolstered front seats designed for high-speed desert running. More aggressive side support than standard JL.
  • 392: The 6.4L Hemi V8 model gets unique front seats with different bolstering to match the more aggressive driving character. Different lateral support geometry than standard JL.
  • Rear seats: Rear bench on Mojave and 392 may use standard JL or JLU dimensions depending on body style. Confirm with Bartact for rear fitment.
