Do seat covers void the Jeep warranty?

No. Aftermarket seat covers do not void your Jeep factory warranty under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act.

JL vs JLU — do the same seat covers fit both?

Front covers fit both JL (2-door) and JLU (4-door). Rear covers are different — always specify 2-door or 4-door when ordering rear seat covers for the JL/JLU.

Are Bartact covers worth the price?

Yes — for anyone who actually uses their Jeep off-road. Bartact's mil-spec Cordura and custom-cut patterns are in a completely different class from anything on Amazon. For daily-driver-only Jeeps, Smittybilt or Covercraft deliver great value on Amazon.
