#2 — Budget Alternative

Generic Paracord Grab Handles

Amazon is flooded with paracord grab handle knockoffs. Some are decent, most are mediocre. The paracord is thinner, the weave is looser, and they tend to slip on the roll bar more than Bartact's. If budget is your #1 concern, they'll work — but you'll notice the difference if you've ever held a Bartact.

Pros
  • Significantly cheaper
  • Many color options available
  • Easy to find on Amazon
Cons
  • Thinner paracord — not always genuine 550
  • Looser weave, less comfortable grip
  • Tend to slip and need re-tightening
  • Color fading in UV

#3 — Rigid Option

Metal/Aluminum Grab Handles

If you prefer a rigid handle, several brands make billet aluminum grab handles for the JL. They bolt to the A-pillar or sport bar and offer a more traditional grab point. Different vibe than paracord — more utilitarian, less colorful.

Pros
  • Extremely durable — metal construction
  • Fixed position — won't move
  • Clean, industrial look
Cons
  • Cold in winter, hot in summer
  • More complex install
  • No color customization
  • Can scratch roll bar
