2024+ Jeep Wrangler JL/JLU — Power Seat Option

Starting with the 2024 model year, Jeep offered an optional power/electric seat on the Wrangler JL and JLU. If your 2024+ Wrangler has the power seat option, the seat controls sit differently than on a manual seat — specifically the side adjustment controls and lumbar switches.

Note: If your 2024+ Wrangler has manual seats (no power option), standard JL seat covers fit identically to 2018–2023 models. This page only applies to the optional power seat configuration.

2024+ Power Seat vs Manual Seat — What Changes for Covers

  • Side controls: Power seat has adjustment buttons/switches on the seat side that some covers can interfere with or cover up
  • Seat base: Power seat motor assembly can change the under-seat clearance and base geometry slightly
  • Lumbar control: If equipped with lumbar adjustment, the control placement matters for cover fit
  • What doesn't change: The seat back, headrest, and general cushion dimensions are the same as 2018–2023

How to Tell if Your 2024+ Has Power Seats

  • Look for adjustment switches on the outboard side of the seat base (driver side left, passenger side right)
  • Check your window sticker or build sheet for "Power Driver Seat" or "8-Way Power Seat" option
  • Manual seats have the traditional lever under the front of the seat
